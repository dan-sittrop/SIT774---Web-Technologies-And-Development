Introduction to Geometric Images

Geometric images are fascinating representations of various shapes and patterns that often exhibit symmetry, repetition, and intricate designs. These designs are used in art, architecture, and various other fields to create visually appealing and thought-provoking visuals.

Geometric Image Details

The images displayed above include various geometric patterns:

  • Mandala: A circular design that often represents unity and harmony. It's a complex pattern used in various cultures for meditation and decoration.
  • Wave Pattern: Features flowing, undulating shapes that create a sense of motion and fluidity.
  • Checkerboard: A pattern of alternating squares, often used in visual design to create contrast and structure.

A rosette images below features a symmetrical, circular pattern that resembles the shape of a flower or a starburst. It often has a radial design, where elements radiate outward from a central point.
